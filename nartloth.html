Inspiration for Artloth

I have always loved Lord of the Rings. My favorite parts of the Fellowship of the Ring include Tom Bombadils story and the elven realm of Lothlorien. In fact I love Lothlorien so much that I have an aribnb attached to my house that I have named Lothlorien. This painting is one that I created for the airbnb that represents our home nesteled in the forest of Lothlorien.
