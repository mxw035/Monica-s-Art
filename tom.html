Inspiration for Tom Bombadil

I have always loved Lord of the Rings. My favorite parts of the Fellowship of the Ring include Tom Bombadils story and the elven realm of Lothlorien. In fact I love Tom's part of the story so much that I will often just play those chapters on audible while out for a walk in the woods. I have always been a bit upset that they cut him from the movies so I painted this to remind me to be more like Tom. To lovve every creature and person that crosses my path and to go throught life with a joyful attitude singing as I go.
