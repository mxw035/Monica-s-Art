About Me

I grew up on a ranch in Texas. It was a small town so there were not many places for me to learn or experience art. We did have a art class once a week at school and I looked forward to it every week. In highschool my family moved to a bigger city and I was able to take a more serious art class and compete in local competitions. I have never been the best artist but I love the process. I now mostly draw/paint in my spare time using a ipad and procreate. I love this because I can enjoy creating art wherever I am. I also enjoy computer science and have been working on desinging websites. This is one of my first websites and I am excited to add this medium to my art portfolio. Thanks for stopping by.
